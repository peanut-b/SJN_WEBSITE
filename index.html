Welcome to San Juan Nepomuceno Parish, a living testament to faith, resilience, and heritage. For generations, our church has been a spiritual home, witnessing prayers, celebrations, and devotion. May our bells continue to call the faithful to worship and serve as a beacon of hope in our community.

Parish Priests

Meet our dedicated clergy serving the San Juan Nepomuceno Parish community.

Parish Priest

Rev. Fr. John G. Barro

Parish Priest & Spiritual Director

Father John has been serving our parish for 8 years with dedication and compassion. He completed his theological studies at the University of Santo Tomas and was ordained in 2005. He specializes in youth ministry and family counseling.

Ordained:

June 15, 2005

Assigned:

July 2016

Education: STL, University of Santo Tomas

Contact: fr.john@sjnp.org

Assistant Parish Priest

Rev. Fr. Michael Rodriguez

Assistant Parish Priest & Liturgical Coordinator

Father Michael joined our parish in 2020. With a background in liturgical music and sacramental theology, he brings a deep sense of reverence to our celebrations. He oversees all liturgical activities and sacramental preparations.

Ordained:

March 20, 2012

Assigned:

January 2020

Education: MA in Liturgical Studies, San Beda College

Contact: fr.michael@sjnp.org

Specialty: Liturgical Music, Sacramental Theology

Parish Vicar

Rev. Fr. Anthony Santos

Parish Vicar & Social Action Director

Father Anthony specializes in social justice and community outreach. He leads our parish's social action programs and coordinates with various community organizations. He has served in missionary work for 5 years before joining our parish.

Ordained:

December 8, 2010

Assigned:

June 2018

Education: MA in Social Justice, Ateneo de Manila University

Contact: fr.anthony@sjnp.org

Specialty: Social Justice, Community Outreach, Counseling
